Frequently asked questions
How can I reach Niva? ▼
The easiest way is through the Contact tab on this website. Once your enquiry is accepted, you can also message me directly through the Messages tab. I'll always get back to you within 24 hours.
What experience do you have? ▼
I'm Red Cross certified and have experience caring for kids from 1 year old to 10. I handle the full routine — dinner, homework help, bath time, and bedtime stories. I've also done late evening jobs up to 11pm.
What happens if I need to cancel? ▼
Life happens! Just message me as early as possible so I can adjust my schedule. I appreciate as much notice as you can give — ideally at least a few hours ahead of time.
Do you do a meet-and-greet first? ▼
Yes, always! Before I start with any new family, I like to do a brief meet-and-greet so your kids can get comfortable with me and you can ask any questions. It's free and low pressure.
How much do you charge? ▼
My rate is $18 per hour. I'm available Sunday through Friday until 11pm. Wednesday and Thursday I'm available outside of 2–6pm.
Can you handle multiple kids? ▼
Yes! I'm comfortable with multiple kids at different ages. I've cared for siblings before and know how to manage different bedtimes, needs, and energy levels.
How do I pay you? ▼
I accept cash at the end of each job. I charge $18 per hour, so it's always easy to calculate in advance — no surprises! I'll let you know the total before the end of the evening so you can come prepared.
